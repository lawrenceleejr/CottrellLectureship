Every **college and university in the US and Canada** is a dot on this map. The ones in
**terracotta** are home to at least one **Cottrell Scholar** — natural partners for a
collaborative lectureship. Institutions that have **hosted a lectureship visit** are marked
with a **teal pin** — tap one to see the talks it hosted. Tap any institution to see its scholars, **search** by name,
city, or scholar, or set a **radius** around a point to list every nearby campus. Open
**Filters** to narrow by **institution type** (R1, R2, R3, master's, PUI, and more) or by
**enrollment size**, and use the legend to show only Cottrell Scholar institutions.
